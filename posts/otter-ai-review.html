What is Otter.ai?

Otter.ai is a popular cloud-based transcription service that uses AI to convert speech to text. It offers real-time transcription, keyword search, and collaboration features. While it works well for general use, its reliance on cloud processing makes it unsuitable for sensitive or confidential audio.

Key Features

🎯 Good Accuracy

Achieves 90-95% accuracy for clear audio with good speakers.

🔍 Smart Features

Keyword search, speaker identification, and summary generation.

📱 Cross-Platform

Works on Mac, Windows, iOS, Android, and web browsers.

👥 Collaboration

Share transcripts and collaborate with team members.

Privacy Concerns

⚠️ Important Privacy Notice

Otter.ai requires uploading all audio to their cloud servers for processing. This means:

  • Your audio data is stored on Otter's servers
  • Privacy policies allow data sharing with third parties
  • Not suitable for confidential meetings, legal recordings, or HIPAA-protected information
  • Potential security risks if servers are compromised

Pros & Cons

✅ Pros

  • Good transcription accuracy
  • Real-time transcription
  • Collaboration features
  • Free tier available

⚠️ Cons

  • Cloud processing = privacy risks
  • Subscription-based pricing
  • Slower than offline alternatives
  • Limited customization

Recommendation

Otter.ai is a good choice for non-sensitive, general-purpose transcription where privacy isn't a concern. However, for confidential audio (legal, medical, business), we strongly recommend offline alternatives like Scriber Pro that process locally on your device.
