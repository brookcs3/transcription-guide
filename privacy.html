Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: September 23, 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

Mac Transcription Guide is committed to protecting visitor privacy. As advocates for privacy-first software, we practice what we preach by minimizing data collection and maximizing transparency about our practices.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain technical information necessary for website operation:

Data Type Purpose Retention
IP Address Security, analytics, spam prevention 30 days
Browser Type Technical compatibility 30 days
Page Views Content optimization 90 days
Referrer URL Understanding traffic sources 30 days

Voluntarily Provided Information

We only collect personal information when you voluntarily provide it:

  • Contact Forms: Email address and message content when you contact us
  • Comments: Name and email if we implement a commenting system in the future
  • Newsletter: Email address if you subscribe to updates (currently not implemented)

How We Use Information

Primary Uses

  • Website Operation: Ensuring proper functionality and security
  • Content Improvement: Understanding which articles are most helpful
  • Communication: Responding to your inquiries and feedback
  • Security: Protecting against spam, abuse, and security threats

Analytics

We use privacy-focused analytics to understand website usage patterns. Our analytics:

  • Do not use cookies or persistent tracking
  • Aggregate data without identifying individual users
  • Focus on content performance, not user behavior tracking
  • Do not share data with third-party advertising networks

Information Sharing

We Do Not Sell Personal Information

Mac Transcription Guide does not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited Sharing Scenarios

We may share information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Security Threats: To investigate potential security violations or abuse
  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors who help operate our website (hosting, email)
  • Business Transfer: In the unlikely event of a business sale or merger

Third-Party Services

Our website may use these third-party services:

  • Web Hosting: GitHub Pages (privacy policy: github.com/privacy)
  • DNS/CDN: Cloudflare (privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacy)
  • Email: For contact form processing (encrypted in transit)

Cookies and Tracking

Minimal Cookie Use

Mac Transcription Guide uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • No Advertising Cookies: We do not use cookies for ads or cross-site tracking
  • No Social Media Tracking: No Facebook, Twitter, or other social media pixels

Browser Controls

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies (may affect functionality)
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

Data Security

Protection Measures

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted to our site is encrypted
  • Secure Hosting: Website hosted on secure, monitored infrastructure
  • Regular Updates: Software and security patches applied promptly
  • Access Controls: Limited access to any collected data

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary:

  • Contact Inquiries: 2 years for reference and follow-up
  • Analytics Data: 90 days maximum, then automatically deleted
  • Technical Logs: 30 days for security and debugging

Your Rights

User Controls

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we have collected
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your information

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@mactranscriptionguide.com with:

  • Your specific request
  • Sufficient information to verify your identity
  • The timeframe or scope of your request

We will respond within 30 days of receiving a valid request.

Children's Privacy

Mac Transcription Guide does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

International Users

Mac Transcription Guide is operated from the United States. If you are visiting from outside the US, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements including:

  • Lawful basis for data processing
  • Data minimization principles
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Data portability rights
  • Prompt breach notification

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Significant changes will be highlighted prominently on our website
  • We will provide reasonable notice of material changes

Your continued use of the website after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@mactranscriptionguide.com
  • General Contact: hello@mactranscriptionguide.com
  • Website: Contact Page

Our Commitment

As advocates for privacy-first software, we understand the importance of protecting personal information. This policy reflects our commitment to transparency and responsible data handling. We welcome questions and feedback about our privacy practices.
